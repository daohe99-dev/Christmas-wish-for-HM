How to make it yours

  1. Drop your primary photo and four funny pictures into the assets/ folder (or assets/photos/ if you prefer keeping them grouped).
  2. Open script.js and update the photos filenames if you used names beyond the defaults like Primary Picture and Funny 1.
  3. Tweak the captions, memories, promises, and wish text to sound exactly like you.
  4. Share the page link or open it together for a surprise moment.
